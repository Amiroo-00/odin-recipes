How to make gravy

ingredients

  • Bacon Fat
  • Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Milk

the recipe

  1. Make the Roux

    Melt bacon fat in a skillet.
    Add flour, salt, and pepper.
    Whisk until the roux is thick and golden.

  2. Add the milk

    Gradually whisk in the milk, then bring to a simmer.

  3. cook until thick

    Continue to cook and stir until the gravy is heated through,
    smooth, and thick. If the gravy is too thick,
    add more milk until it reaches your desired consistency.
